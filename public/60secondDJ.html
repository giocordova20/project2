What is 60 Second DJ?

60 Second DJ is a fast-paced music player party experience that allows the user to choose their own musical adventure in a 60 minute, 60 song power hour of fun! 60 Second DJ utilizes playlists that you create to play 60 seconds of your favorite songs, and gives you a new song every minute.

If you're looking for a mix to start a party, if you need a soundtrack for your workout, or if you just have a really short attention span, then 60 Second DJ has you covered. Wanna see how many songs you and your friends can jam out to on your road trip to Coachella? Just plug 60 Second DJ into your AUX cord and get festival season started early! Don't spend thousands of dollars on expensive mixing equipment and a vinyl collection that only your pretentious hipster friends care about. Just use 60 Second DJ for all your party-starting needs!

How it Works

60 Second DJ combines a cutting edge proprietary ERMAHGERD technology (Extremely Radical Music App to Help Get Everybody Ready to Dance) with Spotify's Web API so that users can keep the party going and the good times rolling for all 60 minutes of their musical adventure. You can start your experience using one of 60 Second DJ's pre-curated playlists from our team of tastemakers and rump shakers, you can import your own pre-made playlists from your Spotify account, or you can create your own here on 60 Second DJ! The party starts as soon as you press play, and every 60 seconds, you'll be punched in the face with a fistful of fun.

Playlists Go Here

Here is the heart of the 60SecondDJ application. Search songs and create your own specialized playlists to fill an hour listening enjoyment. Find and select the songs you think will get your friends talking and laughing. Choose wisely! Don’t want to take the time to search and select 60 songs, get the party started with our pre-populated playlist that has been tested thoroughly by are staff and test participants. Click the Select button next to the playlist name and you’re ready.

Feeling bold enough to create a playlist? First enter a playlist name, and click ‘Create Playlist’. Make sure it’s something that pops. Select the your playlist and you’re ready to start adding songs. Enter a song title and click ‘Search’. Your song will appear in the playlist. Once your list is completed you’re ready to Shot!fy.
