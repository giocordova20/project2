What is Shotify?

Shotify is a fast-paced music player party experience that allows the user to choose their own musical adventure in a 60 minute, 60 song power hour of fun! Shotify utilizes playlists that you create to play 60 seconds of your favorite songs, and gives you a new song every minute! The concept of a "power hour" is a time-tested tradition in the world of partying and pre-gaming with your friends. Now Shotify does all the hard work for you! All you have to do is pick a playlist and sit back, relax, (or stand up and dance), and crack a cold one with your friends.

Every time the song changes, take a drink! The traditional amount is one "shot" of beer every minute for an hour, and who are we to mess with tradition! This ancient formula provides the user with 90 ounces of liquid refreshment in just one hour! (That's 7.5 12-ounce beers for you math fans) Please remember to drink responsibly. If you can't keep up, try alternating with shots of water to prolong the fun!

How it Works

Shotify combines a cutting edge proprietary Alcohol Intake Management System technology (AIMS) with Spotify's Web API so that users can keep the party going and the alcohol flowing for all 60 minutes of their musical adventure. You can start your experience using one of Shotify's pre-curated playlists from our team of tastemakers and rump shakers, you can import your own pre-made playlists from your Spotify account, or you can create your own here on Shotify! The party starts as soon as you press play, and every 60 seconds, you'll be punched in the face with a fistful of fun.

Remember to stick with the formula! Shotify AIMS to give you a good time using the 1:1 drink a minute formula. Our research staff has spent years conducting field tests and have concluded that increasing the drink ratio may seem like a good idea, but could actually impact your experience in a less than ideal way. Please drink responsibly. If you're having too good of a time and don't think you can play for the full hour, it's recommended that you drink some water and watch an episode of Shark Tank. It is not recommended that you start texting exes or send any work-related emails during or after using Shotify.
